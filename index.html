About me

I am a third year PhD student in evolutionary ecology at L'Institut des sciences de l'evolution (ISEM), University of Montpellier in France. I am broadly interested in understanding concurrent eco-evolutionary dynamics. Currently, I am working on my PhD thesis with Dr. Emanuel A. Fronhofer, Dr. Vasilis Dakos and Dr. Oliver Kaltz on eco-evolutionary dynamics of host-parasite systems in complex landscapes. I use simulation and theoretical methods.
